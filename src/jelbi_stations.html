THE stations.

The movement movement.

Jelbi stations bring sharing services to S-Bahn and subway stations. Car, bicycle or scooter: no matter which you choose, here you can hire, return and charge vehicles. Jelbi stations also act as stops for taxis and on-demand shuttles. All in one place at the Jelbi station.

Here, you can select a vehicle to meet your needs and conveniently book it in the Jelbi app. It means a seamless door-to-door experience, e.g. taking the underground to a Jelbi station and switching to a bicycle for the last mile home.

We want to roll out Jelbi stations all over Berlin, from the city centre to the suburbs, so that in the future you can easily change to e-mopeds, bikes, e-scooters or cars at most S-Bahn and subway stations.
